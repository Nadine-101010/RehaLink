# Privacy Policy for RehaLink

RehaLink is a research application intended for use in a clinical study by authorized healthcare professionals and study participants only.

## What data the app processes
RehaLink processes research and health-related data entered by clinicians or study staff for enrolled patients. This may include information such as clinical measurements, observations, and study identifiers.

No data is collected for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

## Where data is stored
All data entered into RehaLink is stored on a secure local server operated by the research institution or clinic conducting the study.

The app communicates only with this local server and does not transmit data to any third-party services, cloud providers, or external platforms.

## Devices
RehaLink is used only on iPads provided and managed by the clinic or research institution. The app is not intended for personal devices.

## Data access
Only authorized study personnel can access the data. Access is restricted to users who are part of the approved research study.

## Data retention
Data is retained according to the policies of the research institution and the approved study protocol.
